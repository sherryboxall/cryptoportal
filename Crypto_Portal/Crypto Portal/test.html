Crypto Projects

At the time of writing, there are an estimated 10,000 crypto currencies and counting.

That's a lot of coins!

Part of the reason this count is so high is because sites such as https://vittominacori.github.io/erc20-generator/ make it ridiculously easy for practically anyone to create their own token in an attempt to reap those crypto dollars. While there are many good tech projects out there, the ease at which a token can be created, coupled with lack of regulation, has led to many, many tokens which are nothing more than money grabs, some of which fill investors ears with empty promises, others don't even bother to do that much! So how is the average retail investor to choose? Which projects are safe, what will stand the test of time and which projects will leave investors holding the bag?


That's what The Crypto Portal was created to discover! To be clear, this site was not created to offer investment advice. I am simply a tech student in love with crypto. Now, I could spend my free time getting balls to bounce, but I prefer to work on projects a bit closer to my heart. All charts are created from chart.js frameworks using messari API calls. They exist to give me valuable coding experience, and just maybe make me a better investor...

I hope you enjoy!
